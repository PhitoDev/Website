The winds were crackling

Shaking the windows 

And the railings 

For what seemed like forever 

And then the calm came, 

And the banging of the rocks 

Against the shutters 

Were absent 

And the idea of “forever”

Lost its sting

We lose things 

In that kind of stillness 

The dark humor

About our imminent demise 

From the storm of course 

That made us desperate for stillness 

And for each other 

Now gone 

Making space for restlessness 

And then the calm finally left

The winds aren’t like before; 

They are now bringing the treeline down 

It’s here again– 

If you’re still chasing
